Instructions

  1. To start, click on "Make Bet" to make a bet.
  2. Then press "Deal" to start.
  3. Press "Restart" to start a new round when the round is over.
  4. Remember, you can always press "Advice" if you need help on whether or not to hit.

Atlantic City Blackjack Rules(8 decks)

  • Dealer must hit if the hand score is 16 or under. Dealer will stand on anything 17 or above, including soft 17s (Ace and 6)
  • Natural blackjack will be paid 6-to-5
  • Player can only double down on a combined hand of 9, 10, or 11
  • Player may only split once
  • Player may double after splitting
